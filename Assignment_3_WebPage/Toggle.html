Data Analysis

Data Analysis is a process of gathering raw data and then analysing it and from the data, some useful information could be formed and that information could be much useful for the business in various ways like for taking future decisions, solving errors and much more.

As the number and size of the data in any business is increasing day-by-day as the business are also getting wider, data analysis is more useful nut expensive process for any business.

Steps

For the process of data analysis firstly collection than preparation and at last analysis of the collected data is done.

It has an important impact in todays business world for the business growth and increasing profits of the business by making effective decisions.

Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages like better decision making, Improvement of customer satisfaction and many more benefits occurs by data analysis.

And as we know that where there are advantages there will always be some disadvantages. So here some disadvantages like over reliance on data, security risk, ethical concerns and more can be found.
